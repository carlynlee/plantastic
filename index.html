About

Mary Lee’s love for plants blossomed after she immigrated from the Philippines to the U.S. in the 1980s. Settling in her new home, she found joy in transforming her patio into a lush green haven. Over the years, Mary has nurtured a vibrant collection of houseplants, each with its own story and charm.

Now, as she embarks on new projects, Mary is ready to share her beloved plants with others. She’s selling her well-cared-for greenery to make room for fresh ideas and a new chapter in her plant journey. Each plant she offers comes with the same love and care that has defined her gardening over the decades.

Mary hopes to help you "Find Your Fronds Forever," bringing a little piece of her passion for plants into your home.
